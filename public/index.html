HOW TO PLAY

⚔ BATTLE BASICS

  • Each player takes turns — answer a quiz question first.
  • ✓ Correct — your attack is powered up. Choose any action!
  • ✗ Wrong — you can only Guard this turn. No attacks or items.
  • All players see the question while someone is answering.

⚡ STEAL THE TURN

  • When someone answers wrong, a 10-second steal window opens.
  • Any other player can buzz in and answer the same question.
  • First correct stealer takes the turn — but ATTACKS ARE HALVED!
  • If nobody steals in time, the original player guards.

🔋 TEAM SYNC GAUGE

  • Every correct answer fills your team's Sync Gauge.
  • 4 correct answers = 100% (counts both teammates combined).
  • At 100%, unleash your ULTIMATE move (needs a correct answer too!).
  • Hitting an enemy's elemental weakness gives bonus gauge.

🎒 ITEMS

  • Each player starts with 3 items — only usable on a correct answer:
  • 💊 HP Boost — restore 500 HP to one ally
  • ⚡ SP Boost — restore 150 SP to one ally
  • 💫 Revive — bring back a KO'd teammate at 50% HP
